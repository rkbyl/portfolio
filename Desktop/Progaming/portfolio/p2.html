About Me

Hello, and welcome to my website! I'm Richard Byler, a first-year student at the University of Michigan School of Information, located in beautiful Ann Arbor. My conception of where I want this program to take me is still evolving, even on a day-to-day basis, but I am currently interested in the areas of preservation and information analysis and retrieval. I'm still early in my information education though, so I look forward to further exploring those areas—and more—in subsequent classes and though a variety of hands-on learning opportunities. The future seems very exciting indeed!
